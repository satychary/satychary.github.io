Apps for the iPhone

In Jan' 2007, Steve Jobs and Apple launched the 'smartphone revolution'...

Prior to that, there was the quaint notion that a 'phone' was MOSTLY for making... wait for it...a 'phone call' :) There _were_ simple apps that came with the earlier flip-phones - GPS, mp3 music playing, read .txt (!) files, etc. But THIS was non-existent!

Today, a smartphone can be a book, encyclopedia, wallet, weather station, clock, radio, navigator, TV broadcast station, radio/TV/cable receiver, game system, word processor, art canvas, VR/AR viewer, shopping catalog... and a phone too :) 'Apps' are what made this possible.
